**Christiane Druml** is a bioethics expert and chairwoman of the Austrian Bioethics Commission. She is also the Vice Rector for Clinical Affairs at the Medical University of Vienna. She studied law at the University of Vienna and obtained her PhD in the field of international law. From 1992 until October 2011 she was Managing Director of the Ethics Committee of the Medical University of Vienna.
