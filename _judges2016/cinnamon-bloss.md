**Cinnamon Bloss** is an Assistant Professor at the University of California, San Diego and an adjunct policy analyst at the J. Craig Venter Institute. Her research interests focus on the individual and societal impacts of emerging biomedical technologies. Major projects have included studies of direct-to-consumer genomics, clinical genome sequencing, biosensors and mobile health, and personal health big data.
